We are a Chennai based Young Charitable Trust founded in 2015 by a group of like-minded people who have been living for decades in and around Thirurvalluvar Nagar, Kodungaiyur, Chennai.

What are we doing?

We have come together and formed this trust with the motto of Empowering the lives of Children and Elderly in whatever way we can. We are aspiring to help the two most vulnerable sections of society, Children and Elderly.

We also pledge to improve the environment by planting trees and educating people about the importance of keeping our surroundings clean.

Inspiring sustainability and advancement of communities in and around our neighborhood.

Our Roots Grow Deep

Today, our charity is a slowly but steadily growing organization that partners with local people, foundations and schools in their efforts to improve the ecosystem and communities through tree planting projects, education, and living standard development programs.

Success Stories

Our charity volunteers visited Truth Foundation orphange for children and celebrated Deepavali with children by donating Dresses and sponsoring Lunch.

When unprecedented floods ravaged Tamil Nadu last year, it affected millions especially in Chennai and surrounding areas and Cuddalore. Volunteers from our trust went to a hamlet in Cuddalore and distributed clothes to the people and the elderly in need.

Interested in Donating?

There are so many great ways to get involved. Volunteer your time or simply provide a financial donation. Every rupee makes a difference.

Give with Purpose

  • Children - A recurring gift of Rs.500 a month allows two children to receive a 30 day supply of nutrition.
  • Elderly - A recurring gift of Rs.500 a month allows two seniors to receive 30 day supply of nutrition.
  • Study - A recurring gift of Rs.1000 a month allows two students to receive a study materials.
  • Food - A recurring gift of Rs.500 a month allows essential food relief to a family in crisis.
  • Your one-time donation gives us the confidence to address the issues that we must take care of immediately. Our goal is to steward your gift with the greatest of care.
