Who We Are

We are a Chennai based 
charitable trust.
We are a Chennai based Young Charitable Trust which was formed in 2015 by a group of like-minded people who have been living for decades in and around Thirurvalluvar Nagar, Kodungaiyur, Chennai.
We have come together and formed this trust with the motto of Enriching the lives of Children and Elderly in Need in whatever way we can. We are aspiring to help the two most vulnerable sections of society, Children and Elderly.
We also pledge to improve the environment by planting trees and educating people about the importance of keeping our surroundings clean.
